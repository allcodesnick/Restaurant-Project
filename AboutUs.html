About Us



History
Established in St. Joseph, MO, in 1993, La Mesa Mexican Restaurant is the Midwest's favorite Mexican restaurant. Over the years, we've grown to operate eight restaurants across Omaha, NE, Council Bluffs, IA, Lansing, KS, and St. Joseph. Owner Frank Onate and general manager Jose Salazar have both been working in the restaurant industry for more than 25 years. Their experience in all aspects of restaurant operation, from creating quality meals to managing multiple locations, is what makes La Mesa the best Mexican restaurant around!
Quality Service
At La Mesa Mexican Restaurant, we want every customer to leave with an excellent dining experience. We work hard to ensure every authentic Mexican dish and cocktail we serve is properly prepared with fresh ingredients. We also strive to provide great customer service, whether that's getting your food out in a timely manner or addressing any concerns you may have. When quality food meets quality service, we know we've done our job right.
